1. Team Introduction

1.1 About Us

Simple Volleyball Team was founded on a shared love for the game. We believe that volleyball, at its core, is about precision, teamwork, and the relentless pursuit of excellence. Our squad is built on the foundation of mutual respect and a collective drive to improve every single day.

1.2 Our Coaches

Travis

Travis is a Level 1 Local Volleyball Coach certified by the VBAHK. He specializes in fundamental skill development and systematic training. His coaching philosophy emphasizes precision in technical execution and team strategy, ensuring every player builds a solid foundation for advanced play.

Hei

Hei is an experienced volleyball player registered under the VBAHK. With years of competitive experience on the court, he excels at guiding players from an athlete's perspective, focusing on tactical positioning and mental toughness during high-pressure matches.
